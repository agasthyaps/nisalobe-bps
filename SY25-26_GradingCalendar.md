# BPS 2025-2026 Report Card Schedule

> **Source:** Boston Public Schools
> **School Year:** 2025-2026
> **Edition:** 08.20.2025
> **Context:** This document defines the grading windows and report card distribution dates for schools using either a **Trimester** or **Quarter** schedule.

---

## Trimester Schedule

Used by schools on a three-term grading cycle.

> **Note:** Kindergarten students do not receive a Fall report card.

### Term Dates

| Trimester | Start Date | End Date |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Fall** | September 4, 2025 | December 5, 2025 |
| **Winter** | December 8, 2025 | March 20, 2026 |
| **Spring** | March 23, 2026 | June 22, 2026 |

### Progress Reports (Mid-Term Check-In)

| Trimester | Grading Window | Distribution Date |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Fall** | October 20-24, 2025 | October 24, 2025 |
| **Winter** | February 2-6, 2026 | February 6, 2026 |
| **Spring** | May 11-15, 2026 | May 15, 2026 |

### Report Cards (End-of-Term)

| Trimester | Grading Window | Distribution Date |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Fall** | December 1-12, 2025 | December 15, 2025 |
| **Winter** | March 16-27, 2026 | March 30, 2026 |
| **Spring** | June 9-18, 2026 | June 18, 2026 |

## Quarter Schedule

Used by schools on a four-term grading cycle. Includes a separate accelerated timeline for **seniors** in Term 4.

### Term Dates

| Quarter | Start Date | End Date | Senior End Date (Term 4 only) |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------------------------------|
| **Term 1** | September 4, 2025 | November 7, 2025 | -- |
| **Term 2** | November 10, 2025 | January 30, 2026 | -- |
| **Term 3** | February 2, 2026 | April 10, 2026 | -- |
| **Term 4** | April 13, 2026 | June 22, 2026 | **June 5, 2026** |
